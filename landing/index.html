Questions?

Here's what families usually ask.

How private is my data?

Your household data and inventory stay 100% private and encrypted. We never sell your data. Read our full privacy policy.

How does the vision scanning work?

Upload a photo of your fridge or pantry. Our AI recognizes items, estimates quantities, and detects expiration dates. You can manually edit anything.

Can I override suggestions?

Yes! Swap ingredients, exclude items, or regenerate plans. KitchenIQ learns from your preferences over time.

Do you handle dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Set allergies, intolerances, dislikes, and dietary preferences (vegan, keto, etc.) for each household member.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, no questions asked. You won't be charged if you cancel before the next billing date.

What if I don't have a pantry photo?

You can manually type ingredients. Or start with suggested staples and build from there. Vision scanning is just a shortcut.
